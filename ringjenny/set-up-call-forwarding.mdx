# 📞 Forwarding Calls to Jenny

Jenny is always ready to answer your calls—but when she's on the clock is totally up to you. You control when Jenny is active by turning call forwarding on or off for your business phone number. Forwarding your calls to your custom RingJenny number lets her answer calls, respond to customers, and handle things on your behalf—without changing your existing number. The RingJenny team will never interrupt your current service, and we'll always notify you by email before you get close to any overage charges. Read through the guide below to understand how call forwarding works and how to set it up for your specific phone provider.

 

## 🔍 What Is Call Forwarding?

Call forwarding is when your phone sends incoming calls to another number automatically—like your RingJenny number. Once it's set up, your original business number stays the same, but Jenny handles the calls in the background.

 

## 🔁 Types of Call Forwarding

There are two main types of call forwarding:

**Unconditional Forwarding** Forwards all calls to Jenny—immediately and automatically.

**Conditional Forwarding** Only forwards calls in specific situations:

- **If busy** (when you're already on the phone)
- **If no answer** (when you don't pick up)
- **If unreachable** (when your phone is off or has no signal)

 

> ⚠️ If you want Jenny to handle more than one condition (like both busy and no answer), you'll need to set each one up separately using its own code.
>
> Not all carriers support conditional forwarding.

 

## ⚙️ How to Set Up Call Forwarding

Most carriers use special dial codes to set up forwarding and it will look like something similar to the steps outlined below:

1. Open your phone's dialer (like you're making a call).
2. Enter the setup code plus your RingJenny number. For example: `**21*1234567890#`
3. Press **Call**.
4. You'll usually hear a confirmation tone or message.

> ⚠️ **IMPORTANT: Setup codes vary by carrier.** Be sure to scroll down and follow the specific instructions for your carrier below to ensure Jenny works correctly.

 

## 📵 iPhone Users: Turn Off Live Voicemail

If you're using an iPhone, be sure to turn off **Live Voicemail**. This feature can prevent Jenny from receiving forwarded calls when you don't answer—especially if you're using conditional forwarding.

To turn it off:

1. Open the **Settings** app
2. Tap **Phone**
3. Tap **Live Voicemail**
4. Toggle it **off**

Jenny works best when your iPhone isn't trying to screen or answer calls on its own.

 

## 🔁 How to Turn Off Call Forwarding

To turn off call forwarding, you'll either dial a special code from your phone or disable it in your provider's app or settings.

**To disable all forwarding:**

- Dial your carrier's cancel code (like `##21#` or `*73`) and press **Call**.

**To disable specific conditions (for GSM networks like AT&T and T-Mobile):**

- `##67#` disables forwarding when busy
- `##61#` disables forwarding when no answer
- `##62#` disables forwarding when unreachable

> 🧠 **Heads Up: Not all carriers support these specific disable codes.**
>
> The codes above work for most GSM networks—like AT&T, T-Mobile, and providers like Cricket, Metro, and Mint.
>
> Verizon and Boost Mobile don't support condition-specific disable codes. Use `*73` to turn off all forwarding instead.
>
> Google Voice, RingCentral, and other VoIP services require you to manage forwarding in their app or website.
>
> When in doubt, check your carrier's section below for exact instructions.

# Popular VoIP & Web Phone Systems

 

## ➤ OpenPhone

📖 _Full setup instructions from OpenPhone support_

**Unconditional Forwarding (All Calls):**

1. Click **Settings** from the left-hand menu
2. Under **Workspace**, click **Phone Numbers**
3. Select the phone number you want to forward
4. In the **Call flow** section, select **Forward all calls**
5. Add your RingJenny number and click **Forward**

**Conditional Forwarding:** To forward calls only at specific times or if unanswered:

_For forwarding outside business hours:_

1. Go to **Call flow** settings
2. Click **Business hours**
3. Under **Outside business hours**, add the RingJenny number you want to forward to

_For forwarding during business hours (unanswered calls):_

1. In **Call flow**, scroll to the **Unanswered calls** section
2. Under **When no one answers**, select **Forward to another number**
3. Enter your RingJenny number

**To Disable Forwarding:**

- Return to your call flow settings and remove or turn off the forwarding rule.

**Notes:**

- OpenPhone supports granular routing based on time of day and availability.
- Forwarding settings apply per phone number.

## ➤ Google Voice

**Unconditional Forwarding (All Calls):** To forward calls from Google Voice to Jenny, you'll need to link your RingJenny number and verify it.

1. Log into your **RingJenny Admin** and go to **Account \> Account Settings**
2. Enable the **"Call Forwarding Verification - SMS authentication code"** toggle

You can set up and configure call forwarding from the Google Voice desktop browser or from within the Google Voice device app.

**For Desktop:**

1. In a new tab, go to Google Voice
2. In the top right, click the **Settings** gear icon
3. Under **Account \> Linked numbers**, click **New linked number**
4. Enter your RingJenny number and click **Send code**
5. Return to the **RingJenny Admin \> Account Settings** tab and click **View text messages**
6. This will open a window displaying any verification codes sent to your RingJenny number
7. Back in Google Voice, enter the code received and click **Verify**
8. Once verified, go to **Settings \> Calls**
9. Under **Incoming calls**, ensure **Call Forwarding** is enabled for your linked RingJenny phone number
10. Return to the RingJenny Admin and disable the verification code toggle

**For Device App:**

1. Open the Google Voice app on your device and go to settings (three-bar hamburger menu in the top left corner)
2. Tap into **Devices and numbers**
3. Tap **New linked number**
4. Input your RingJenny phone number and uncheck the **"number belongs to this device"** option
5. Click **Send code**
6. Return to the **RingJenny Admin \> Account Settings** tab and click **View text messages**
7. This will open a window displaying any verification codes sent to your RingJenny number
8. Remember to disable the verification code toggle
9. Back in the Google Voice app, enter the code received and tap **Verify**
10. Google Voice automatically enables call forwarding when you link a number, however it's best to double check
11. In the Google Voice app, tap back out of **Devices and numbers**
12. Still within settings, tap into **Incoming Calls**
13. Ensure the call forwarding toggle is enabled for your RingJenny phone number

**Conditional Forwarding:** Not supported. Google Voice only allows full (unconditional) call forwarding.

**To Disable Forwarding:**

- Toggle it off under **Settings \> Calls \> Incoming calls**
- Or go to **Settings \> Linked numbers** and remove the RingJenny number

**Notes:**

- You must verify your RingJenny number before Google Voice will forward to it.
- Setup must be completed via web browser, not the mobile app.
- Call forwarding options vary depending on if you have the paid or free version.
- If your Google Voice number is displaying on all of your RingJenny calls, ensure you have the appropriate caller ID setting disabled in Google Voice.
